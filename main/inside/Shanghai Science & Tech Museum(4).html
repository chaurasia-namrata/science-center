Theme

The theme is "Nature ·Mankind ·Technology" with permanent and temporary exhibitions spread over four floors and a basement. The four theatres (including IMAX 3D & IMAX Dome, a Space Theatre and IWerks 4D) show a range of movies on everything from prehistoric history through the modern world to the world of the future. The exhibitions have a lot of interactive displays that delight the child in all of us, and the real children a lot. The science is fascinating, educational and great to explore.


  • IMAX three-dimensional giant-screen theater is one of the world’s most advanced theaters and the first three-dimensional giant-screen theater in mainland of China.

  • IWERKS four-dimensional theater will give you an experience of Disney, and its impact requires your great courage.


  • In IMAX ball-screen theater, you will be shrouded in a huge globe and you will feel as if you were suspended in the air.
